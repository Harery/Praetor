## The Autonomous Production Readiness & Audit System

> **P**roduction **R**eadiness, **A**udit, **E**vidence, **T**esting, **O**perations, & **R**eview

**Praetor** is an enterprise-grade, single-prompt orchestration framework that transforms any codebase into a comprehensive production readiness and audit package. By dispatching **18 autonomous domain experts** overseen by a **4-Judge Quality Council**, Praetor performs multi-disciplinary audits with 100% source-code traceability.

No assumptions. No hallucinations. Just mathematically precise, file-line cited evidence package for engineering, compliance, operations, and business stakeholders.

---

## 🏛️ Core Pillars of Praetor

### 1. 100% File-Line Traceability
Every single issue, risk, test case, or control map generated by Praetor carries a verified citation (`file:line`). The Quality Council automatically double-checks and rejects any finding that cannot be traced to concrete lines of code.

### 2. Swarm Architecture (18 Specialized Personas)
Unlike generic single-agent code reviews, Praetor deploys a hierarchical organization of specialized agents. Each agent possesses a custom 12-20 year industry persona and unilateral authority over their respective domain (SRE, Compliance, OWASP Security, WCAG Accessibility, etc.).

### 3. Multi-Audience Deliverables
Praetor automatically formats its output into dedicated streams, ensuring every department receives exactly what they need in their native syntax.

```mermaid

    A[1. MASTER_PROMPT] --> B[2. Phase 0-2 Discovery]
```

1. **Paste Master Prompt**: Initiate the orchestrator swarms inside your LLM context.
2. **Technical Discovery**: Autodetect language stacks, frameworks, databases, and monitoring libraries.
3. **MUST CONFIRM Gate**: Stop-point requiring explicit user confirmation and parameter tuning.
4. **Agent Swarm Swivel**: Parallelized execution of specialized analysis mandates.
5. **Quality Council Review**: 4 distinct judges check for Coverage, Citations, Clarity, and Skip-Validity.
6. **Release Gate Output**: Emits the structured markdown readiness package.

## 🚀 Quick Start Guide

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Praetor runs inside long-context LLM environments (like Claude 3.5 Sonnet). No complex software downloads or API key configurations are required to get started.

### Step 1: Copy the Orchestrator
Open [prompt/00-orchestrator/MASTER_PROMPT.md](prompt/00-orchestrator/MASTER_PROMPT.md), select all content starting from the `═══` separator line, and copy it.

### Step 2: Initialize the System
Paste the prompt into your AI context, appending a reference to your target codebase:
```
[Paste Praetor Master Prompt]

Source Codebase: https://github.com/your-organization/your-repository.git
```

### Step 3: Confirm & Execute
Once **Phases 0–2** complete (usually under 30 seconds), review the **Discovery Report** and respond using the `CONDITIONAL_CONTINUE` format:
```markdown
continue with:
  Q1 = "React Frontend with Node/Express Backend"
  Q2 = "Requires SOC2 compliance"
override:
  RUN_PRIORITIES = [P0, P1]
then continue
```

## 🏛️ The 18 Agents

### Tier 1 — Discovery & Domain Mapping
* **`A01` Discovery** — Principal Software Archaeologist (20y exp)
* **`A02` Domain Mapping** — Domain-Driven Design (DDD) Architect (15y exp)
* **`A03` Tooling Discovery** — DevOps & CI/CD Tooling Integrator (12y exp)

### Tier 2 — Quality & Robustness Specialists
* **`A04` Unit Test** — Senior Test Automation Engineer (12y exp)
* **`A05` Integration Test** — Integration & System Test Lead (15y exp)
* **`A06` Security** — OWASP Top 10 Security Specialist (18y exp)
* **`A07` Performance** — Performance, Scaling & Volumetric Architect (15y exp)
* **`A08` Accessibility** — Accessibility & WCAG Compliance Expert (10y exp)
* **`A09` Chaos** — SRE Chaos & Resiliency Lead (12y exp)

### Tier 3 — Business & Operations Leaders
* **`A10` Business Analyst** — Senior Business Analyst & Requirements Lead (15y exp)
* **`A11` UAT** — Senior User Acceptance Testing Specialist (12y exp)
* **`A12` Runbook** — Staff Site Reliability Engineer & Runbook Architect (15y exp)
* **`A13` Alerting** — Observability & Alerting Specialist (12y exp)

### Tier 4 — Governance, Compliance & Communications
* **`A14` Support Triage** — Helpdesk & Customer Support Lead (12y exp)
* **`A15` Customer Comms** — Customer Communications Lead (10y exp)
* **`A16` Compliance** — Compliance and Audit Director (18y exp)
* **`A17` Risk** — Risk Management Lead (15y exp)

* **`QC` Quality Council** — 4 independent judges validating **Coverage**, **Citations (100% correct)**, **Clarity**, and **Skip-Validity**.
